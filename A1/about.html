About

ENGG Week is a historic week long engineering spirit event. The week focuses on charity fundraisers, with past years raising upward of $10000.
Departmental teams compete in various events focused around engineering spirit and tradition, with the main goal of charity awareness.

ENGG Week takes place during the first week of classes in the second term of each semester. This usually means it falls right in the middle of January. We hope to see you there in 2020!
