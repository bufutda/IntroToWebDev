Event Overview

  1. Key Clue
    Key Clue is a race between the department teams to solve puzzles. Two alumni hide a key in Calgary and make a series of clues teams must solve to find it. Every ENGG Week Key Clue is themed, so your clues could be doing anything from solving a murder to finding hidden treasure. Whether you’re great at solving puzzles, or even just enjoy brain teasers, Key Clue is a great chance to build your problem-solving skills and develop some great friends.
  2. Opening Ceremonies
    Teams will rendezvous in the ENGG Lounge and reveal their theme and charity.
  3. Parade
    It’s time to let the campus know it’s ENGG Week. Funding issues and apathy aside, ENGG Week is here! As we march on, chants will be shouted through the halls of the various buildings on campus.
  4. Scavenger Hunt Briefing
    Acquire items on the list of required supplies. Your collected items will be assessed during the evauluation event.
  5. Trivia Night
    Think you know all there is to know about engineering? Prove it! Line up head to head with all competing departments to see who knows their history, pranks, and obscure equations best.
  6. Scavenger Hunt Evaluation
    Performance on the collection of the items in the scavenger hunt list be recorded.
  7. Food Sculptures
    Time to showcase your artistic prowess. Your team will be given limited time and resources to build a non-perishable food item sculpture. The sculpture must match the department’s theme. Sculptures will be on display for the duration of ENGG Week.
  8. Games Night
    Looking for a quiet evening to wrap up your first Monday back to school? We got you covered. Take some time off from your classes, bring your favourite board game to the ENGG Lounge and let’s hang out!
  9. Floor Hockey Tournament
    Alright let’s get physical. No? You’ll need some backup ASAP to take out the tango in some good old-fashioned hockey.
  10. Iron Chef
    We have detected a ingredient invaluable to your life as a student - a constant, from coast to coast. Allez cuisine!
  11. Design Challenge
    You're caught in a bind. You must design your way out of this. Arrive in the ENGG Lounge to receive your challenge as well as some required components. Best solution wins. Good luck.
  12. Carnival Day
    Curious where ENGG Factor went? Don’t you worry, you’ll find your challenges here. Not competing as your team’s champion? Don’t worry! Come hang out, play some carnival games. Who knows, maybe there’ll even be a dunk tank… All proceeds from carnival day will go to the winning charity.
  13. Alumni Amazing Race
    It’s time you meet your predecessors. Each department will be given a challenge by each alumni. Arrive at the final meeting place. First team to complete all challenges wins! Meet in the ENGG Lounge at 19:00 to receive your clues.
  14. Day of Service
    Competitors will be sent to a new site to complete a new challenge. Apply now!
  15. Movie Night
    We have received some video transmissions from each department. Stay tuned for message decryption. The most popular event of the week, you should show up early to grab a seat!
  16. Closing Ceremonies
    Time to go to sleep, you have some classes to catch up on.
